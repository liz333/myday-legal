隐私政策

生效日期：2026-07-18
开发者：Lizzy（lizzy@myday.app）
应用：「时间去哪了」（My Day）

本政策说明我们如何处理与本应用相关的信息。我们以「本地优先」为原则：活动记录、标签、目标、计划等核心数据默认保存在您的设备上。

一、我们处理的信息

  1. 您创建的内容：活动记录、标签、目标、计划、日记/今日一句、照片附件、打卡与洞察状态等，默认存储在本机。
  2. 设备权限相关数据（仅在您授权后使用）：
    • 麦克风与语音识别：用于语音输入记录活动；识别在设备本地进行，我们不会将录音或转写上传到我们的服务器。
    • 照片/相机：用于为活动添加照片，照片保存在本机。
    • 屏幕使用时间（Screen Time / Family Controls，若您启用）：用于导入本机屏幕用时数据到日历；数据在本机处理。
    • 通知：用于计划提醒等，仅在您开启相关功能后使用。
  3. 购买与订购：订阅与终身购买通过 Apple 的 App Store / StoreKit 处理。我们不会收到您的完整支付卡信息；我们可能在本机保存订购状态、档位与到期日等权益信息，以便解锁功能。
  4. 账号（若您使用登录）：可能包括邮箱等您提供的账号信息，用于验证与同步相关能力（以您实际启用的功能为准）。

二、我们如何使用信息

  • 提供、维护与改进应用功能（计时、日历、目标、计划、洞察、订购权益等）。
  • 在您授权的前提下使用系统能力（语音、照片、屏幕用时、通知）。
  • 履行购买与恢复购买相关的必要处理。

三、存储、传输与第三方

  • 核心用户内容默认本地存储。若您启用云同步或账号相关服务，数据可能按该功能的说明在您的账号体系中传输/存储。
  • 语音识别使用 Apple 系统接口；在部分系统配置下，Apple 的语音识别可能需要网络。我们不会另行把音频上传到自有服务器。
  • 支付与订购由 Apple 处理，并适用 Apple 的相关政策。
  • 我们不向广告商出售您的个人数据。

四、数据保留与删除

  • 本机数据由您控制。您可在设置中清空本机数据（订购权益可能按产品规则保留）。
  • 卸载应用通常会删除本机数据（以系统行为为准）。
  • 与 Apple 订购相关的管理、取消与退款，请通过 Apple ID / 系统「订阅」设置处理。

五、儿童隐私

本应用不以儿童为主要受众。若您认为我们无意中收集了儿童的个人信息，请联系我们以便处理。

六、政策更新

我们可能更新本政策。更新后会调整生效日期；继续使用即表示您知悉更新后的政策（法律另有要求的除外）。

Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-18
Developer: Lizzy (lizzy@myday.app)
App: My Day (“时间去哪了”)

This Policy explains how information related to the App is handled. We follow a local-first approach: core content such as activity records, tags, goals, and plans is stored on your device by default.

1. Information we process

  • Content you create: activities, tags, goals, plans, journal / daily sentence, photos, check-ins, insight states, and similar data — stored on-device by default.
  • Permission-related data (only after you grant access):
    • Microphone & Speech Recognition: used for voice logging; recognition runs on-device; we do not upload audio or transcripts to our servers.
    • Photos / Camera: used to attach photos to activities; photos stay on-device.
    • Screen Time / Family Controls (if enabled): used to import on-device screen-time data into the calendar; processed on-device.
    • Notifications: used for plan reminders and similar features when enabled.
  • Purchases: subscriptions and lifetime purchases are processed by Apple App Store / StoreKit. We do not receive your full payment card details. We may store entitlement status, plan, and expiration locally to unlock features.
  • Account (if you sign in): may include email and related account data you provide, for authentication and sync features you enable.

2. How we use information

  • To provide and improve App features (timer, calendar, goals, plans, insights, entitlements).
  • To use system capabilities you authorize (speech, photos, screen time, notifications).
  • To support purchase and restore flows.

3. Storage, transfer, and third parties

  • Core content is local by default. If you enable cloud sync or account services, data may be transmitted/stored under those features.
  • Speech uses Apple system APIs; depending on system settings, Apple speech recognition may require network. We do not separately upload audio to our own servers.
  • Payments are handled by Apple under Apple’s policies.
  • We do not sell your personal data to advertisers.

4. Retention and deletion

  • You control on-device data. You may clear local data in Settings (subscription entitlement may be retained per product rules).
  • Uninstalling usually removes on-device data (subject to the OS).
  • Manage, cancel, or request refunds for Apple subscriptions via Apple ID / system Subscriptions settings.

5. Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children. Contact us if you believe a child’s personal information was collected inadvertently.

6. Changes

We may update this Policy and the effective date. Continued use means you acknowledge the updated Policy, except where law requires otherwise.
